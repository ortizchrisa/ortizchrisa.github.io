Creative technologist by day. Nerdy illustrator by night.

My name is Chris, and I like to draw tings.

I received my Bachelor of Science in Visual Arts at SUNY New Paltz in 2012. After I graduated, I worked as a graphic designer for political think-tanks, ad-agencies, and app developers. Currently, I work at the NYC advertising agency, mcgarrybowen, where I animate, develop, and design digital advertisements.

I spend most of my freetime creating original characters and stories. My love for video games, comics, and animation influences my personal projects, in hopes that I may someday tell tales that are as beloved as those that inspire me.
